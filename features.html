Features at BROCHETA LOS CUERNUDOS

Live Cooking Stations:

Guests can watch chefs prepare brochetas at open grills, offering an engaging and educational culinary show.

Signature Brocheta Challenges:

Monthly competitions where guests can create and submit their brocheta recipes for a chance to be featured on the menu.

Eco-Friendly Takeaway Packaging:

Biodegradable and compostable packaging for takeout orders, adorned with traditional Mexican art.
